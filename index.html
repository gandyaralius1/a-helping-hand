Welcome to A Helping Hand

A Helping Hand Re-Entry Program is a community-based organization dedicated to supporting formerly incarcerated individuals as they transition back into society. We believe everyone deserves a second chance, and with the right structure, accountability, and support, long-term success is achievable.

Our Mission

To empower returning citizens with the tools, resources, and guidance needed to rebuild their lives, strengthen families, and reduce recidivism through education, mentorship, employment readiness, and mental health and substance abuse referrals.

Our Vision

A future where returning citizens are welcomed back into society with dignity, opportunity, and purposeâ€”creating safer communities and breaking cycles of incarceration.

Our Programs

12-Week Intensive Core Program

  • Orientation and individualized goal setting
  • Life skills and decision-making development
  • Accountability and discipline coaching
  • Mental health awareness and emotional wellness education
  • Substance abuse education and recovery referrals
  • Job readiness and resume development
  • Interview preparation
  • Financial literacy basics
  • Weekly check-ins and progress tracking

12-Month Full Re-Entry Program

  • Ongoing mentorship and case management
  • Employment placement assistance
  • Job retention support
  • Housing guidance and community referrals
  • Transportation assistance when available
  • Mental health and substance abuse treatment referrals
  • Access to counseling and recovery partners
  • Financial growth planning
  • Family reintegration support
  • Community leadership development

Mental Health & Substance Abuse Support

We recognize that mental health challenges and substance use disorders are often connected to incarceration and re-entry barriers. A Helping Hand provides referrals to licensed mental health professionals, substance abuse treatment providers, outpatient and inpatient recovery programs, and community-based counseling services.

Disclaimer: A Helping Hand Re-Entry Program does not provide clinical or medical treatment. All mental health and substance abuse services are referral-based through licensed third-party partners.

Employment Pipeline

Through partnerships with SAJAA Investments LLC, participants gain access to workforce opportunities in skilled labor, construction, maintenance services, and small business employment pathways.
